Our Story

Why We're Running

We are three friends who decided to do something a lil loco, sign up for the Berlin Marathon !!

We're fundraising for the the Foundation, which funds research, education, and quality of life programs for those living with Cystic Fibrosis. CF is a life-threatening genetic disease affecting the lungs and digestive system, and every dollar we raise helps bring the CF community closer to a cure

Our goal is to raise $5,250 before our deadline. Every single donation, large or small, makes a real difference. Thank you thank you thank you from the bottom of our hearts <3
