Our Story

Why We're Running

We're three friends who decided to do something a little crazy — sign up for the Berlin Marathon — and use every step as an opportunity to raise money for a cause close to our hearts.

We're fundraising for the Boomer Esiason Foundation, which funds research, education, and quality-of-life programs for those living with Cystic Fibrosis. CF is a life-threatening genetic disease affecting the lungs and digestive system, and every dollar we raise helps bring the CF community closer to a cure.

Our goal is to raise $5,250 before our deadline of September 25th. That's less than $200 per mile — and every single donation, large or small, makes a real difference.
